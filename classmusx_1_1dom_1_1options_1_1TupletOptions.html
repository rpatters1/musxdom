enum class musx::dom::options::TupletOptions::NumberStyle

Number style options.

Note
Finale appears to have implemented RatioPlusBothNotes and RatioPlusDenominatorNote backwards from how they are named in the xml. It is probably a long-standing bug that was never worth fixing. This DOM corrects the mistake by reversing their mapping in the enum mapping.
